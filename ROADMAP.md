# RealCity Builder - Next 20 Development Steps

Inspired by Cities: Skylines, SimCity, Anno 1800, Factorio, and others. Prioritizing gameplay loop, depth, and polish.

## Core Simulation & Economy (Steps 1-5)
1. **Demand & Happiness System** - Residential/commercial demand based on services. Happiness affects growth (inspired by C:S).
2. **Resource & Goods Chain** - Basic production (water/power → goods). Factories, storage.
3. **Budget & Taxes** - Monthly income/expenses UI. Adjustable tax rates.
4. **Population Needs** - Food, education, healthcare tiers. Upgradable buildings.
5. **Disaster Events** - Random fires/floods (power/water grid impact).

## UI & Controls (Steps 6-10)
6. **Build Menu & Tool Modes** - Radial/toolbar with categories (zones, infrastructure, services).
7. **Minimap + Camera Controls** - Zoom, pan, rotation. Fog of war optional.
8. **Info Overlays** - Density, happiness, power/water coverage heatmaps.
9. **Notifications & Tutorials** - Popups for low funds, unmet demand.
10. **Pause / Speed Controls** - 1x, 2x, 3x simulation speed.

## Advanced Features (Steps 11-15)
11. **Road & Traffic Simulation** - Pathfinding, congestion (basic agents).
12. **Public Services** - Police, fire, schools, hospitals as placeable buildings.
13. **Modular Building Upgrades** - Visual levels + bonuses (e.g. +pop, efficiency).
14. **Trade & External Economy** - Import/export ports, global market prices.
15. **Terraforming & Environment** - Raise/lower terrain, pollution spread.

## Polish & Expansion (Steps 16-20)
16. **Day/Night + Seasons** - Dynamic lighting, weather effects on grids.
17. **Blueprints & Copy/Paste** - Save/load building clusters.
18. **Achievements & Milestones** - Unlock new building types.
19. **Multiplayer / Scenarios** - Basic hotseat or challenge modes.
20. **Audio & Visual Polish** - Particle effects, sound (construction, traffic), OSM integration polish.

Prioritize steps 1-8 for a solid playable alpha. Each step should include save compatibility bump.
